ABOUT

I am currently finishing my education at Epicodus, a 6-month full-time vocational school for aspiring programmers. Prior to Epicodus, I earned my degree in Environmental Science through the University of Oregon's Robert D. Clark Honors College. I spent several years working and interning for the Port of Portland's Environmental Operations department, learning a lot about environmental regulatory work in the context of airport, seaport and industrial operations. Experiencing the technology-gap in the workplace, I saw an opportunity to learn an entirely new set of skills and focus on solving stimulating problems as a web developer. After many months of intensive training focused on Front-End Development and Design, I'm thrilled to continue learning new principles, languages, and frameworks while beginning my career making beautiful, fully-functioning websites and applications.
